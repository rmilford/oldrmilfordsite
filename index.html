A Little Background
Artist + Techy + Storyteller = Rebekah

My name is Rebekah Milford and I am a digital designer who loves to creatively blend graphical design, video production, and web design to craft compelling narratives and messaging strategies.

I have a strong background in communications, political campaigning, and marketing, but it wasn't my passion.

Fortunately I am a self-starter and quick learner. I taught myself to use Adobe Studio to make my own art, edit photos, and create cool videos. I also taught myself front end and full stack development. Since then I've created my own e-commerce business, designed my own products, sold my products on multiple e-commerce market places, and built (and automated) by B2B relationships to simplify production, payment, and fullfillment.

I am looking for new and interesting collaborations.

In my freetime, I love playing with my dog POTUS, traveling, painting, listening to podcasts, reading and practicing yoga.
